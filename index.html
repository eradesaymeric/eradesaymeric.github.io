I'm a Ph.D. student working on computer vision and visualizations at LIRIS/Ecole Centrale de Lyon supervised by Romain Vuillemot . My work focus on how to improve performances of professional player using matches broadcast. I am working on three main issues:

  • How to extract data from matches broadcast
  • How to analyze extracted data
  • How to create easy to understand visualizations
All my research work is applied to table tennis in partnership with the french table tennis federation (FFTT) to help the French nationnal teams to understand outcomes of matches and help them to prepare futher matches.
